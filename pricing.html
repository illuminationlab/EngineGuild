How usage-based pricing works

Pay only when the phone rings.
Or the text sends. Or the AI replies.

Every feature that costs real money when you use it — phone calls, text messages, outbound emails, AI minutes — is billed for what you actually use. No inflated packages. No “unlimited” tiers that roll the cost into a giant flat fee. A Voice AI call that books a $150 tune-up costs pennies. A review-request text that earns you a five-star review costs about the same as a stamp. You pay a little when it works, and nothing when it doesn’t.

Phone calls (inbound + outbound)

Per-minute on LC Phone or Twilio numbers. Call recording and tracking are included in the base.

Voice AI minutes

Per-minute voice engine cost plus token-based LLM usage. A typical booked tune-up call costs pennies. Requires Growth or Professional.

Conversation AI tokens

Token-based pricing. Each chat reply is a fraction of a cent. Requires Growth or Professional.

SMS & MMS

Pay per message across SMS, WhatsApp, and Messenger. Transparent, competitive rates. Available on every tier.

Outbound emails

A monthly email pool is included. Past that, usage-based pricing kicks in — stays cheap even at high volume.

Paid ad spend

Google, Facebook, and Instagram ad spend you push through the Ad Manager is billed directly by the ad platforms. Professional tier only.
