Voice AI

Your 24/7 AI receptionist.

Your phone never rings into a void again. Voice AI answers inbound calls with a natural, human-sounding voice you customize to match your shop. It greets callers and handles the common questions — hours, pickup-and-delivery area, typical tune-up timing, do-you-service-this-brand, how-much-for-a-carb-rebuild.

  • Books, reschedules, and cancels appointments on your live calendar — it sees open slots and offers them.
  • Creates a customer record with name, phone, email, and what machine they called about.
  • Sends a follow-up text with a confirmation link, directions, or a payment link.
  • Transfers to you when a caller asks for the owner or the situation needs a human.
  • After-hours rules you control — book the job, send to voicemail, or forward to your cell.
  • 200+ voices — pick the tone, the pace, and the personality.

Conversation AI

Website chat + multi-channel messaging bot.

Conversation AI runs your website chat widget and replies to inbound SMS, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, and WhatsApp — all from one unified inbox. It uses a custom knowledge base you build and edit anytime.

  • Answers questions from your services, FAQs, hours, and policies.
  • Qualifies leads — collects name, phone, email, the machine, and how urgent.
  • Books appointments directly inside the chat.
  • Escalates to you with one click when a human is needed.
  • Understands voice notes — customers tap the mic, explain what’s wrong, and the AI picks up the intent.
  • Suggestive or fully automated — your call, per channel.

Two-way SMS & unified inbox

Every text, DM, email, and chat lives in one inbox. Reply from your phone between jobs. Send a quote, a pickup-ready text, or a “running ten late” note with two taps. Stop switching between five apps to remember what you told which customer.

Missed call text-back

Every call you miss triggers an automatic text: “Hey, this is EngineGuild at Shop Name — we just missed you. Reply here and we’ll get you booked.” The caller stays engaged instead of dialing the next listing. Keep the job that was about to walk to your competitor.

Call tracking & recording

Every inbound and outbound call is logged against the customer record. Recordings are optional but available — useful for walking back through what a customer asked for when a unit is sitting on the bench and you can’t remember the details.

Scheduling & booking

Fill your week before you get out of bed.

Online booking & live calendar

Your booking page lives on your website. Customers pick a service (tune-up, carb rebuild, storage service, winterize, de-winterize, blade sharpening, pickup-and-delivery), pick a time, and confirm. Your calendar updates in real time across every device.

Route-day calendars for mobile service

Set up separate calendars for each service area or route day. A Monday route to the south side, a Wednesday route to the north — the booking system only offers slots that fit the day. Spring rush doesn’t become geographic chaos.

Automated appointment reminders

Text and email reminders fire on your schedule: 48 hours out, 24 hours out, two hours out. Customers confirm with a tap. No-shows drop. You stop rolling up to an empty driveway.

Sales, quotes, and payments

Get paid without chasing checks.

Quotes and estimates

Build a quote in under a minute from a template. Email or text it to the customer with a one-tap accept button. Accepted quotes flow into the job pipeline automatically. Stop writing quotes on carbon-copy forms that get lost in a truck console.

Invoicing and payments

Invoice from your phone while standing next to the unit. Customer taps, pays with a card or ACH, and the invoice closes. Accept deposits before parts are ordered. Stop fronting parts money on a handshake.

Automated payment reminders

Unpaid invoices get friendly automated nudges at the intervals you set: three days late, seven days late, fourteen days late. Each reminder includes a one-tap pay link. Collect what you’re owed without a single awkward phone call.

Document signing

Send service agreements, warranty disclaimers, commercial fleet contracts, or winter storage intake forms for digital signature. Stop chasing paper and keep everything in one customer file.

Automation workflows

Set it once. Never forget to follow up again.

Visual workflow builder

Drag-and-drop automations for the repeatable stuff: new-lead intake, quote-accepted flow, job-closed flow, storage-season reminder, de-winterize reminder in April, off-season nurture. Every workflow you build runs in the background forever.

AI-powered workflow builder

Describe what you want in plain English — “when a customer books a tune-up, text them the intake form, add them to the tune-up pipeline, and email me a heads-up” — and the AI builds the triggers, actions, and IF/THEN logic for you. Automate the shop without learning a new system.

Seasonal nurture campaigns

Drop customers into a winter nurture sequence in October and a spring wake-up sequence in March. Gentle reminders to book winterize service, de-winterize service, or pre-season tune-ups go out on schedule. Fill January with snowblower work and April with mower work.

Marketing

Fill a slow week by noon.

Email marketing & broadcasts

Send a spring tune-up promo to every customer who had a mower serviced last year. Send a snowblower reminder to every customer in a northern zip code when the first frost hits. Track opens, clicks, and bookings.

SMS broadcasts

Short, timely texts to customer segments — “first snow’s coming, get your blower in before the rush,” “we’ve got two slots open Friday for emergency tune-ups.” SMS beats email for open rates every time.

Funnels and landing pages

Drag-and-drop builder for high-converting pages: the seasonal promo page, the commercial fleet contact page, the snowmobile storage page. No web developer needed. Launch a promo the same morning you think of it.

Funnel & website AI builder

Type a prompt — “build me a landing page for winter snowblower tune-ups with a booking form” — and AI generates a complete page. Edit or publish. Skip the blank-page stare.

Content AI

Generate on-brand blog posts, social captions, and email copy from a prompt. Write once, schedule across channels. Show up online without hiring a marketer.

Social media planner

Plan and schedule Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, and LinkedIn posts in one calendar. Seasonal promos, before-and-after repair photos, tune-up tips — all queued in advance. Post when it matters and not at 11pm on a Sunday.

Ad manager (Google, Facebook, Instagram)

Launch and track paid campaigns from the same dashboard. See which ads produce booked jobs, not just clicks. Stop guessing whether your ad spend is working.
