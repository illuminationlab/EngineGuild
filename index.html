How it works

From signup to first booked job
in three simple steps.

Most shops are live within about two weeks. We handle the heavy lifting so you can keep working on engines during the transition.

Step 1 — We set up your shop in the system

We import your customer list, build your service menu (tune-ups, carb rebuilds, storage service, winterize, de-winterize, whatever you offer), and configure your booking calendar to match your route days and shop hours. You’re not figuring it out alone.

Step 2 — You plug in your phone, your email, and your website

Calls route through EngineGuild. The website gets a chat widget. Review requests fire after closed jobs. Missed calls trigger an automatic text. Everything talks to everything.

Step 3 — You work on engines. The system works on the rest.

You get notifications when a job is booked, a quote is accepted, or a customer needs you. The rest happens on autopilot. You stop living inside your phone.
